# MongoDB Fiscal Calendar

Fiscal year runs **Feb 1 – Jan 31**. FY number = calendar year the FY ends.

Always use `FY## Q#` format — e.g., `FY27 Q1`, `FY26 Q4`. Never `Q1 2026` or `Q1`.

| Quarter | Months |
|:--------|:-------|
| Q1 | Feb – Apr |
| Q2 | May – Jul |
| Q3 | Aug – Oct |
| Q4 | Nov – Jan |
